The quarry sites are a joint-enterprise between [Ruben Enterprises LLC](../orgs/ruben.md) and [Benji's Crafting Co](../orgs/benji.md). 

The quarry is also the site of the RuBEN Power Plant. Power can be acquired for a reasonable price.
